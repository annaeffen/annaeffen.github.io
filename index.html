Biography

I'm currently an undergraduate student at Cornell University studying Economics, Statistics, and Computer Science.

I've always been passionate about understanding and shaping the world by creatively applying quantitative methods to data, whether that's studying patterns in natural language or uncovering trends in financial markets.

My projects so far include studying collaborative language at Cornell Tech's Language, Interaction, and Learning (LIL) Lab under Professor Yoav Artzi and quantifying biases in juror selection under Professor Martin T. Wells.
